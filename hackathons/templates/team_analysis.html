The National Level Hackathon on Blockchain is a highly anticipated event under the Digital Transformation theme of C20, which brings together blockchain enthusiasts, experts, and students from across the country to showcase their skills and creativity in developing innovative blockchain-based solutions. During the hackathon, participants will have the opportunity to attend tech talks and interact with industry leaders and mentors to refine their ideas and gain valuable insights into the latest trends and advancements in blockchain technology.
The hackathon will culminate in a final pitching session where the top teams will present their solutions to a panel of judges comprising of industry experts and investors.
The National Level Hackathon on Blockchain is an excellent platform for participants to gain hands-on experience, network with like-minded individuals, and contribute to the digital transformation of the country.

Submission and FAQ

Dates

Application : 22/03/2023 - 23/04/2023

Final Round: 04/05/2023 - 05/05/2023

Round Details

1: Application (Online)

  • Theme : Digital Transformation using Blockchain
  • Submit your profile details and answer a few questions for the initial screening
  • [Optional] Submit an abstract about your idea. It is recommended that the abstract be concise and focused, with a maximum word count of 100.
  • Judges and the panel of experts will shortlist top teams to hack at the offline round at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham - Amritapuri

2: Final Round (Offline - At Campus)

  • Shortlisted teams would be invited to the hackathon via mail to participate in a 30-hour offline hackathon

  • The event will be filled with talks from industry leaders in the field of blockchain and various fun and lively activities to keep the hackers engaged.

  • During the lifespan of the hackathon, teams get to develop a working prototype and pitch the entire project to judges on the final day of the hackathon.
    Teams will also be provided with mentors to help pitch their ideas and final presentation.

  • A panel of industry experts will review the final project, and prizes will be awarded to the top teams.
